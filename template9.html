Logistics Procedure

On-Signers

Crew can fly to Bandaranaike International Airport with an OKTB; an advance VISA is not required. Our staff will meet them at the immigration counter and obtain a special on-arrival seaman VISA.

Crew will then be accommodated in a hotel or arranged meals prior to their departure from Colombo (BIA) airport.

Off-Signers

Crew does not require an advance VISA. We will obtain a special on-arrival seaman VISA for them after disembarkation.

Crew will then be accommodated in a hotel or arranged meals prior to their departure from Colombo (BIA) airport.
