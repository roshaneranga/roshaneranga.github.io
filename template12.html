Crew Change Procedure

On-Signers

• Fly to Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) with an OKTB. Advance VISA is not required.

• Our staff will meet crew at the immigration counter to obtain a special on-arrival seaman VISA.

• Crew will be accommodated in a hotel or arranged meals prior to airport departure.

Off-Signers

• Crew does not require an advance VISA for entry.

• We will obtain a special on-arrival seaman VISA for them immediately after disembarkation.

• Crew will be accommodated in a hotel or arranged meals prior to airport departure.

If you have any questions please do not hesitate to contact us anytime.
