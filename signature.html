Thank you for your kind mail and inquiry. We are pleased to work with your esteemed company. Below is the EPDA and crew change procedure for your kind reference.

Required documents as follows,

01. Passport copies of the on/off signers.
02. Seaman book copies of the on/off signers.
03. Flight ticket copies (we can also assist with booking the tickets if required).
04. Vessel contact details for pre-arrival formalities.

Please find the below procedure,

▲ On Signers

•  Crew can fly to Bandaranaike International Airport with an OKTB and advance VISA is not required. Our staff will meet them at the immigration counter and obtain a special on arrival seaman VISA.

•  Crew will then be accommodated in a hotel or arranged meals prior to their departure from Colombo (BIA) airport.

▼ Off Signers

•  Crew does not require an advance VISA. We will obtain a special on arrival seaman VISA for them after disembarkation.

•  Crew will then be accommodated in a hotel or arranged meals prior to their departure from Colombo (BIA) airport.
