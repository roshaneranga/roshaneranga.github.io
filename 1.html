Operational Directive // Galle OPL

Transit Procedure

DOC REF: SL-GAL-001

This procedure outlines requirements for vessels transferring crew, security personal, and equipment at Galle OPL, Sri Lanka.

Rendezvous Point

Vessel must arrive at the following RV point unless otherwise advised by the master of the crew boat:

Latitude 05° 52' N
Longitude 80° 13' E

Standard Operating Guidelines

  • 01

    Vessel must advise her ETA to designated RV point DAILY and 12, 6, 4, 2 hours basis prior to reaching the RV point.

  • 02

    All equipment, weapons and ammunition must be secured by the security team prior to transfer.

  • 03

    The crew boat will supply the life vests and life vests must be worn by all personnel at all times during the transfer.

  • 04

    Vessel must maintain speed of 2-3 knots while transferring the crew and luggage.

  • 05

    Following crew boats are deployed and vessel can communicate with boat on channel 73 and channel 16:

    PENGUIN 24 PENGUIN ALPHA

Mandatory Requirement: Please ensure to wear protection gear for signing off crew.
