Logistics Protocol

Sign On

• Our staff will welcome the joiners at the airport and arrange a special visa on arrival.

• Crew will be taken to the hotel or port upon arrival in Sri Lanka depending on the vessel ETA.

• After the formalities they can embark to the vessel.

Sign Off

• After crew disembark and arrive at the jetty, our staff will get them an on-arrival special visa at the port.

• Crew will transfer to the hotel or airport based on the departure flight plan.

• Crew can fly out of Colombo airport via Commercial flights ✈️.
